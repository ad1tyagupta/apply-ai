# Evaluate Mode

When evaluating a role:

1. verify geography, limitations, posting language, and language fit
2. compare the role against the profile wiki
3. score the role from `0-100`
4. compare the score against the active threshold
5. explain why the role is a fit or not a fit
6. prepare factual application assets only for shortlisted roles

Reject roles when the user’s stated limitations rule them out, even if the broader profile looks strong.
